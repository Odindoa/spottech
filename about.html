Our Journey

  • 2015 - Founded as Omia Malo Digital Centre

    Started with a mission to promote digital literacy and provide computer services to the East Asembo community. Trained over 20 students and offered consultancy to local schools.

  • 2023 - Expansion to Kisumu and Online

    Expanded services beyond the local community to Kisumu and launched online offerings to reach a wider audience. Rebranded to Spot Tech Cyber Cafe

  • 2024 - Specialization in Cybersecurity

    Shifted focus exclusively to cybersecurity services to address growing digital threats and protect businesses and individuals. Rebranded to Spot Tech Int'l

  • 2025 and Beyond - Vision for the Future

    Aim to develop AI-powered threat detection solutions, expand cybersecurity consultancy across East Africa, and establish research hubs dedicated to cutting-edge security innovation.
